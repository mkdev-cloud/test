Welcome to Trade Flow Tycoon: Global Finance Expedition! 🌍

Embark on an exciting journey as a global finance magnate! Your goal is to navigate the complexities of international trade, secure profitable deals, and manage financial risks to build your empire.

Game Objective

Your main objective is to reach a target profit and successfully complete a minimum number of trade deals. Be careful though, failed deals and poor decisions can lead to bankruptcy or a bad reputation!

How to Play

  • Select Opportunities: Choose from various trade opportunities presented to you. Each deal has different goods, quantities, destinations, and associated risks. You can adjust your investment in a deal, which will impact potential profit.
  • Choose Instruments: Select the most suitable trade finance instrument (e.g., Letter of Credit, Open Account) for each deal. Instruments vary in cost, risk, and speed.
  • Fix Documents: Before executing a trade, review critical documents. Some might contain "bugs" that need to be fixed. Failing to fix them can lead to penalties or failed deals.
  • Execute Trade: Once documents are in order, execute the trade. Active deals will progress over time, and random events might occur, affecting your profit or reputation.
  • Monitor Progress: Keep an eye on your cash balance, total profit, reputation, and active deals. Level up by completing deals to unlock new challenges.
  • Manage Risk: High-risk deals offer higher rewards but also come with greater chances of failure. Balance your portfolio!
  • Events Log: Stay informed with the market events and news log, which provides updates on your trades and any global occurrences.
  • Leaderboard: Compete with other players (or yourself!) on the leaderboard based on total profit and completed deals.

Winning & Losing

Win by achieving a high total profit and completing a set number of deals. Lose by going bankrupt or failing too many deals.
